# Runic Roots

**_Runic Roots_** is a game that combines elements of roguelike deckbuilders
with farming games.

In this game, players manage a magical farm where they grow mystical plants and
herbs imbued with runic energies. The plants on your farm are not just for show;
they provide you with a powerful deck of cards that you can use in combat to
defeat various enemies and bosses.
